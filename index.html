Hello, world!


You have landed on my personal research site, dedicated to various experiments with digital epigraphy and quantitative analysis of inscriptions. I love to think about inscriptions as petrified voices coming from the past, delivering messages from people quite similar to us. I truly believe, that by studying inscriptions in large quantities we can learn not only about our past, but also about our daily lives. I love to talk about inscriptions, archaeology, history or cats. Most days you can find me either staring at the computer screen, drinking coffee in a cool place, hiking outdoors, or in the gym / curling hall!


For more details about me, visit the Curriculum Vitae section, or send me an email.


You can follow me either on Twitter, GitHub or via ORCID.
